# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 16, 2026

Thank you for using the **No-Cost Bot** ("the Bot"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and how it is stored.

## 1. Information We Collect

To function correctly, the Bot collects and stores the following information:

- **Guild (Server) Identifiers:** We store the unique ID of any Discord server where the Bot is configured.
- **Channel Identifiers:** We store the unique IDs of channels designated for notifications.
- **Platform Preferences:** We store your selected preferences for game platforms (e.g., Steam, Epic Games) to filter notifications accordingly.
- **Command Usage Data:** We may log interactions with the Bot (e.g., slash commands used) for debugging and performance monitoring.

We **do not** collect:
- Personal information such as names, email addresses, or physical addresses.
- Message content from your server (the Bot only reads interactions directed at it).
- User-specific data beyond what is necessary for administrative commands.

## 2. How We Use the Information

The collected information is used solely for the following purposes:
- **Notification Delivery:** To send free game deal alerts to the correct channels in your server.
- **Service Configuration:** To remember your server's settings and platform filters.
- **Maintenance & Debugging:** To identify and fix technical issues.

## 3. Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage:** All data is stored in a local SQLite database on the host machine.
- **Third Parties:** We do not share, sell, or trade your server's data with any third parties.
- **Security:** We implement standard security measures to protect the host environment where the data resides.

## 4. Data Retention and Deletion

- **Retention:** Data is kept as long as the Bot is configured on your server.
- **Deletion:** You can remove your server's configuration by using the `/setup` command to clear channels or by removing the Bot from your server. 
- **Requesting Deletion:** You may contact the developer to request manual deletion of your server's data.

## 5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this document.

## 6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out via our [Support Server](https://discord.gg/yarZZ5zeNP).
